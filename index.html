Privacy first

Your data stays on your phone. Full stop.

This is not a tagline. BillCrunch has no servers to talk to.

No account

Nothing to sign up for. No email, no password, no identity. Install and use immediately.

No internet required

The app works fully offline. Everything is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. No network calls, ever.

No tracking

No analytics, no ad SDKs, no telemetry. The app cannot see what you do with it, and we do not want to.

You own the backup

Export a full JSON backup to any storage you choose. Restore it anytime. Factory reset wipes everything locally, instantly.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Is BillCrunch free?

BillCrunch is free to download and free to use. A Premium tier unlocks adding more than six obligations and extends the forecast view beyond the next month. The core tracking, reminders, backup, and export features are available to everyone at no cost.

Does it sync across my devices?

No, and that is intentional. BillCrunch stores all data in a local database on your device. To move data to a new device, export a JSON backup and restore it on the new phone.

What permissions does it need?

Only local notification permissions (for payment reminders), and storage access if you use the backup or export features. It does not request contacts, location, camera, or network permissions for core functionality.

Can I track bills in multiple currencies?

Yes. BillCrunch supports 15 currency symbols including USD, GBP, EUR, AUD, CAD, JPY, CHF, INR, NZD, SGD, HKD, KRW, BRL, MXN, and ZAR. Note that no conversion is performed. Each bill displays in the currency you assign it.

What happens if I pay a different amount than expected?

The Confirm Payment flow lets you log the actual amount paid, recording the variance against what was expected. You can choose to update the forecast with the new amount or keep the original estimate.

Is there an iOS version?

An iOS version is in development. You can follow us on social media for updates, or check this page periodically. The App Store listing will appear here as soon as it is live.

Can I categorise bills my own way?

BillCrunch has six default categories: Property, Subscriptions, Utilities, Insurance, Vehicle, and Personal. Each supports customisable sub-categories. You can also apply optional tags as a second filter layer for finer grouping.

How do I get help or report a bug?

Visit the Support page for guides and contact options. Feedback through Google Play reviews is also read and acted on.

Our principles

Built on the belief that simple tools work.

BillCrunch exists because most personal finance apps are either too complex, too nosy, or too expensive. We think tracking your bills should be straightforward and private by default.

Simplicity over features

Every feature in BillCrunch earns its place. We add things that make the core job better, not things that make the app look impressive on a feature list.

Privacy and data ownership

Privacy is not a setting — it is the architecture. No servers means no leaks. Your data stays on your device. Export it, back it up, delete it. Total control, always.

Honest about pricing

The core app is free. A Premium tier exists for power users who need more than six obligations or a full-year forecast. No dark patterns, no bait-and-switch.
